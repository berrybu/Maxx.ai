## Company and Positioning
**EuroCircuit PCB GmbH** is a high-reliability printed circuit board (PCB) manufacturer for automotive electronics, industrial control, and new-energy applications,
headquartered in Munich, Germany, with dual plants in Germany and Poland. Certified to IATF 16949, UL, and IPC Class 3,
focused on automotive-grade high-density interconnect boards.

## Core Product Lines

### 1. HDI high-density interconnect boards
- Layers: up to 16 layers, any-layer HDI
- Minimum trace width/spacing: 3 mil / 3 mil (75 µm)
- Laser microvias: minimum hole 0.1 mm, blind and buried vias
- Applications: ADAS domain controllers, in-vehicle cameras, mmWave radar modules

### 2. Automotive-grade multilayer boards
- Substrate: FR4 Tg170, polyimide, -40°C to +150°C operation
- Copper weight: 1–6 oz, supports heavy copper / high current (e-drive / BMS)
- Impedance control: ±5% (differential/single-ended), full-board impedance test report shipped with goods

### 3. High-frequency / RF boards
- Rogers RO4350B / hybrid stack-ups
- Dielectric constant Dk controlled to ±0.05, suitable for 77GHz automotive radar
