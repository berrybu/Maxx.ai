# Maxx Components GmbH — Product Technical Doc

## Company Overview
Maxx Components is a German automotive parts manufacturer focused on e-drive, transmission, and sensing systems. IATF 16949 certified.

## Core Product Lines
- MX-eDrive: 800V high-voltage e-drive assembly, peak efficiency 97%.
- MX-Trans: multi-speed electric transmission, fits passenger and light commercial vehicles.
- MX-Sense: ADAS sensor suite (radar + camera fusion).

## Value Proposition
- Per-unit cost reduced by 12-18%.
- Mass-production PPM < 15.
- Average lead time 6 weeks.

## Typical Customer Pain Points
OEMs face supply-chain fragmentation, cost pressure, and quality-consistency challenges during the 800V platform transition.

## Commercial Terms
- Tiered pricing; the larger the annual volume, the lower the unit price.
- Sample delivery within 4 weeks supported.
